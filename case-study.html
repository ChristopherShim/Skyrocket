Full test coverage kept Barter from popping

Barter, a platform for building web apps without code, needed a way to test an infinite number of workflows across multiple environments, including features flagged off in Production. Not a problem! By integrating QA Foundry into their development process, nearly 100% of Barter’s core features had automated test coverage within four months — and without pulling their engineering team off of feature development or building an in-house QA team. Today, every release goes through full UAT and regression and their customers enjoy a stable, reliable platform for their businesses.
